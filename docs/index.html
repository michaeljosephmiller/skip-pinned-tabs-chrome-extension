Welcome to Skip Pinned Tabs

Skip Pinned Tabs is a Chrome extension that allows you to quickly and easily skip over pinned tabs when cycling through your active tabs.

With Skip Pinned Tabs, you can navigate through your open tabs more efficiently, without having to click through all of your pinned tabs every time you switch to a new tab. This extension is perfect for anyone who frequently works with a large number of open tabs.
